Hobbies

Text Adventure Detective Games

I am passionate about detective and suspense-style text adventure games (AVG). Compared to classic detective fictions, AVGs leverage visual elements, music, and simple animations to provide multi-dimensional storytelling capabilities, which perfectly addresses the common issue of boring narratives before puzzle-solving in traditional detective fictions.

Shift-JIS Art Story Creation

Originating from Japan's 2ch text-based forums, Shift-JIS Art is a unique writing medium of ASCII ART (AA). Due to technical limitations of the text board, users create images composed of ASCII characters to assist in storytelling, forming a distinctive internet subculture. This text-based comic format allows people without drawing experience to edit and combine AA from material libraries to create desired text images.

I have also created stories using AA based on my interests, and driven by the desire to read more AA stories, I developed software tools for automatically translating Japanese AA forum posts.
